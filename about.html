About Ledger & Key

Recruiting built around the deal, not the database.

We started Ledger & Key because too much mortgage and real estate recruiting treats producers like line items instead of people with a number that needs to make sense.

Our Story

Why we do this differently

Mortgage and real estate are relationship businesses built on trust, timing, and numbers that actually add up. Most recruiting in this space doesn't reflect that — resumes get blasted to dozens of employers at once, comp conversations happen after an offer instead of before, and confidentiality is an afterthought.

Ledger & Key exists to fix that sequence. We talk to producers first, understand what would genuinely move them, and only then go find the employers who can actually deliver it. No mass submissions, no surprises at the offer stage, and no contact with your current employer unless you explicitly want us to make it.

We work across South Florida and licensed nationwide, placing loan officers, processors, underwriters, and agents with lenders and brokerages that are hiring to grow — not just to fill a chair.

Ledger & Key is founded and operated by Joe DiPerna.

How We Operate

What you can count on, every time.

Confidentiality first

Your search stays private. We never contact a current employer or submit your information without sign-off, role by role.

Numbers before narratives

We lead with production, comp structure, and licensing — the facts that actually decide whether a move makes sense.

A real person, every time

Every submission is reviewed by a recruiter, not a filter. You'll hear back from a human within 48 hours.
