Common Questions

What people ask before reaching out.

Is this confidential?
Completely. We never contact your current employer, and we never submit your information to any lender without your explicit approval — role by role, every time. Your search stays private until you say otherwise.
What does this cost me as a candidate?
Nothing. Our fee is paid entirely by the employer once a placement is made. You pay zero — before, during, and after the process.
Do I need to be actively looking to reach out?
No. Many of our best placements start as "just curious" conversations. If the right opportunity comes up that genuinely improves your situation, we'll bring it to you. If not, there's no pressure and nothing owed.
How quickly does this typically move?
We review every submission within 48 hours and aim to have qualified candidates in front of the right employers within a week of intake. From first conversation to signed offer varies — but we don't let it drag, and we stay in contact the whole way through.
What states are you actively placing in right now?
We're currently filling Branch Manager and Senior Loan Officer roles in AL, AZ, AR, CO, FL, GA, IL, KY, ME, TN, TX, and WI. If you're not in one of those states, reach out anyway — our employer network spans more than our current open listings.
